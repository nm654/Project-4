Knowledge is Power!

The best way for a person to contribute to helping solve the problem of melting glaciers is to first be informed about it. The second way to contribute is learning more about glaciers. Wikipedia is a good place to start, then would be moving onto news sources that are verified and have scientists behind them.

Organizations

Otherwise, the best place to start learning about glaciers would be glacier.org. The next would be the national park service (NPS) which is where I’ve gathered a lot of information on glaciers in order to write this memo. But if information's not enough...

Your Contribution

The next best thing to do is attempt to reduce the amount of greenhouse gases you produce. Carbon emissions can be reduced by being more conscious of things that cause them, like cars. Carpooling would be an example I’d use as something that can be done. Informing others of ways to reduce carbon emissions helps as well. Recycling and using less energy consuming electronic products can take small steps to reducing the gases. But the largest impact to prevent the massive number of emissions would be somehow finding a way for larger companies to stop using all their energy emissions. Unfortunately, that’s something that’s far out of my scope to try and write about how it could be handled.
