Glaciers and the World Around Them

Glaciers may just look like giant blocks of ice floating in the middle of the ocean doing absolutely nothing, but they have their importance even if it doesn’t impact your daily life.


A glacier isn’t even made of just ice! It’s a long history of ice, snow, rock, sediment, and water. They come in multiple types based on their size (sheets, caps, valley, cirque) and location. They are often called “rivers of ice” and were named after the French word “Glace” which means ice.
