Energy from Glaciers

Hydroelectric power is harnessed by melting glaciers in order to generate electricity. There is also the impact of glacier loss on plants, animals, and eventually the economy. Since the threat of melting glaciers is large a lot of research is dedicated towards finding ways to slow down the melting of glaciers and find the cause.

Further Information on Melting Glaciers
